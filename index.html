I'm a Web Designer

Hi there, I hope you're doing well! My name is João Eduardo, and I'm a professional web designer and programmer specializing in solving my clients' challenges. With expertise in creating visually stunning websites and implementing small scripts to enhance your work or life, I aim to deliver out-of-this-world designs and practical solutions tailored to your needs.

How it Started?

When I finished school at 16, I explored various fields such as medicine and law, but none resonated with me as they all seemed monotonous. Seeking something truly captivating, I embarked on a journey. At 18, an opportunity arose to play futsal in Italy, where I stumbled upon something fascinating - programming. Through connections in the field, I delved into learning Java, which, despite not being the ideal choice, sparked my interest immensely. The onset of the pandemic brought me back to Brazil, where my passion for programming flourished. I began my journey with pure HTML and CSS, but soon gravitated towards Bootstrap and Tailwind for their efficiency. Discovering Python was a game-changer; its versatility and simplicity captured my heart. Today, I specialize in building comprehensive websites for businesses of all sizes. Additionally, I leverage Python to create small scripts that streamline repetitive tasks and develop intricate software solutions. Client satisfaction is paramount to me. I prioritize transparency throughout the project lifecycle, maintaining open communication through calls and messages. Every decision is made with the client's best interests in mind, ensuring a seamless and rewarding collaboration.
